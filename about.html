ABOUT

Hello, I'm Jo.

Nice to meet you :)

I've worked in the design and marketing space for the last 10 years, focussing mainly on the digital side. I've worn many hats over my time in this space and consider myself a jack of a very specific subset of trades! From print and web graphic design, to video editing, gif-making, PowerPoint creation, animation, email marketing, social media, content creator...the list continues. I'm a bit of an all-rounder.

Website-wise, I'm pretty proficient using and maintaining WordPress websites. I've dipped my toe in the HTML and CSS waters and like what I see. I have a naturally curious mind, I need to understand the things I do and so I'm a 'serial learner'. I believe that we should never stop learning new things.

Away from the computer I'm a Netflix and fluffy animal enthusiast.
